Shoelace Formula

A = ½ × [(x₁y₂ + x₂y₃ + x₃y₄ + x₄y₁) – (x₂y₁ + x₃y₂ + x₄y₃ + x₁y₄)]

This formula calculates the area of any quadrilateral given the coordinates of its four vertices. The result represents the absolute area value.
