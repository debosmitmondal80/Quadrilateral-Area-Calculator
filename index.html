About the Shoelace Formula

The Shoelace formula (also known as the surveyor's formula) is a mathematical algorithm to determine the area of a simple polygon whose vertices are described by their Cartesian coordinates.

Formula Used:

A = ½ × [(x₁y₂ + x₂y₃ + x₃y₄ + x₄y₁) – (x₂y₁ + x₃y₂ + x₄y₃ + x₁y₄)]

Usage Tips

  • Enter coordinates in clockwise or counterclockwise order
  • Decimal values are supported for precise calculations
  • The formula works for any simple (non-self-intersecting) quadrilateral
  • Result is always positive (absolute area)
